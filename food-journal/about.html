About Food Journal

Welcome to the Food Journal! This is a site that allows you to keep track of what you have eaten throughout the day. You can add a new meal, view your meals, and search through your meal history by date or content.

It's a pretty simple site designed with your health in mind. Whenever you eat, go ahead and add it in here! Whenever you want to see more details about a specific meal, you can search through your entries or click on any meal to see more information.

Our Purpose

This site is meant to help people keep track of what they have eaten throughout the day. This is particularly helpful for people who have stomach problems, where keeping track of what they eat can help them identify problematic foods that might be causing discomfort.

Who This Is For

This site is designed for people who:

  • Have sensitive stomachs or digestive issues
  • Are experiencing stomach problems and want to track potential triggers
  • Are trying to lose or gain weight and need to monitor their eating patterns
  • Want to develop healthier eating habits
  • Are curious about their daily nutrition and meal timing

Key Features

Easy Meal Logging

Quickly add meals with title, date, time, and description. Perfect for tracking what you eat and how you feel afterward.

Smart Search

Search through your meal history by food name or description to find patterns and identify triggers.

Time-Based Filters

View all meals, today's meals, or this week's meals to see your recent eating patterns at a glance.

Detailed View

Click on any meal to see comprehensive details including timing, full descriptions, and additional notes.
